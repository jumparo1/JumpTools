What This Tool Does

Calculates your Bulgarian crypto tax automatically. Import CSVs from your exchanges, it computes FIFO cost basis, classifies every transaction, and generates the exact numbers you need for your annual tax return (Form 200, Appendix 5, Code 508).

Step-by-Step Workflow

Step 1 — Export CSVs from your exchanges

Binance:
• Spot trades: Orders → Spot Order → Trade History → Export
• Deposits: Wallet → Transaction History → Deposit → Export
• Withdrawals: Wallet → Transaction History → Withdrawal → Export
• Futures: Orders → Futures Order → Trade History → Export
• Earn: Wallet → Transaction History → Distribution → Export

Bybit:
• Spot trades: Assets → Spot → Order History → Export
• Futures: Derivatives → Order History → Trade History → Export
• Copy Trading: Assets → Copy Trading → Asset Change Details → Export
• Wallet: Assets → Transaction Log → Export

Kraken:
• Trades: History → Export → select "Trades" → Export CSV
• Ledger: History → Export → select "Ledgers" → Export CSV

Important: Export the FULL YEAR (Jan 1 – Dec 31) for each tax year.

Step 2 — Import into this tool

• Select your tax year from the dropdown at the top
• Drag & drop ALL your CSVs onto the import zone (or click to browse)
• The tool auto-detects which exchange and type each file is
• You can import multiple files at once
• Check the import badges to confirm all sources are loaded

Step 3 — Review & classify

• Go to "Needs Review" tab
• The tool auto-matches transfers between exchanges (e.g. Binance withdrawal → Kraken deposit)
• Unmatched items need your input: click "Transfer", "Taxable", or "Buy" to classify
• Red-flagged items = missing cost basis (tool treats as €0 — worst case for you)

Step 4 — Verify the numbers

• Go to "Tax Summary" tab
• Check: Spot gains/losses, Futures P&L, Combined net, Tax owed
• Cross-check against your exchange account summaries
• If futures classification is unclear, your accountant will advise

Step 5 — Export for your accountant

• Go to "Export" tab
• Download Приложение 5 — ready-to-fill values in BGN (Code 508)
• Download Accountant Cover Letter — Bulgarian summary with your name
• Download Full Ledger CSV — every transaction for audit trail
• Send all files + original exchange CSVs to your accountant

Step 6 — File with NRA

• Go to nra.bg
• Годишна данъчна декларация по чл. 50 → Формуляр 200
• Fill in Приложение 5, Част I, Таблица 2, Код 508
• Enter: Колона 3 = продажна цена, Колона 4 = цена на придобиване
• The system auto-calculates Колона 5 (печалба) or Колона 6 (загуба)
• 10% expense deduction is applied automatically
• Pay the 10% tax owed
• Deadline: April 30 (file by March 31 electronically = 5% discount up to 500 лв)

Key Concepts

Bulgarian Crypto Tax Rules

Tax rate: 10% flat on net realized gains (individual)
10% expense deduction: Automatically reduces your taxable income by 10% — no proof needed (чл. 33, ал. 3 ЗДДФЛ)
Effective rate: 9% (10% tax on 90% of gains)
Taxable events: Selling crypto for fiat, swapping crypto-to-crypto, paying with crypto
NOT taxable: Holding, self-transfers between own wallets/exchanges
Losses: Offset gains within same year only — NO carry-forward to next year
Professional trading (15%): If NRA classifies your activity as systematic (3+ trades) — discuss with accountant

FIFO Cost Basis

FIFO = First-In, First-Out. When you sell crypto, the tool matches it against your OLDEST purchase first.

Example: You buy 1 BTC at €30,000, then buy 1 BTC at €40,000. You sell 1 BTC at €50,000.
→ Gain = €50,000 - €30,000 = €20,000 (uses the oldest lot)

The tool tracks all your acquisition "lots" and consumes them in order.

Transfer Detection

When you move crypto between your own exchanges (Binance → Kraken), it's NOT a taxable event.
The tool auto-detects these by matching: same asset, similar amount (±2%), within 24h, different exchanges.
If it can't match automatically, you'll see it in "Needs Review" — just click "Transfer" to classify it.

Your Flow: Binance/Bybit → Kraken → Revolut

Trade on Binance & Bybit (taxable when you close positions)
→ Withdraw crypto to Kraken (self-transfer, NOT taxable)
→ Sell for EUR on Kraken (taxable disposal)
→ Withdraw EUR to Revolut (fiat movement, NOT taxable)

The tool tracks each step and classifies correctly.

Supported CSV Formats

10 formats auto-detected

Binance: Spot trades, Deposits, Withdrawals, Futures, Earn/Distribution
Bybit: Spot trades, Copy Trading, Futures/Derivatives, Transaction Log
Kraken: Trades, Ledger (deposits/withdrawals/staking)

Just drop any CSV — the tool figures out what it is. If it can't detect the format, you'll see an error message.

How to file (Как се подава)

1. Go to nra.bg → Годишна данъчна декларация по чл. 50
2. Fill in Приложение 5, Част I, Таблица 2, Код 508
3. Enter the values from the Appendix 5 export above:
  • Колона 3 (Продажна цена) = Total disposal proceeds in BGN
  • Колона 4 (Цена на придобиване) = Total acquisition costs in BGN
  • Колона 5 (Печалба) = Net gain in BGN (if positive)
  • Колона 6 (Загуба) = Net loss in BGN (if negative)
4. The system auto-applies the 10% expense deduction (чл. 33, ал. 3 ЗДДФЛ)
5. Pay 10% tax on the result
6. File by March 31 electronically for 5% discount (up to 500 лв)
