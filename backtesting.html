💡 Write however you want — the parser understands all of these:

"oversold RSI, sell overbought" → buy when RSI below 30, sell when RSI above 70
"MACD crossover strategy" → buy when MACD crosses above signal, sell when MACD crosses below signal
"mean reversion with Bollinger, 3% stop" → buy when price below lower BB, sell when price above upper BB, SL 3%
"golden cross + high volume, TP 20%" → buy when SMA 50 crosses above SMA 200 and volume 2x, TP 20%
"go long 20 MA crosses 50 MA, SL 5%" → buy when SMA 20 crosses above SMA 50, stop loss 5%

🔧 Supported indicators

RSI — RSI below 30, RSI above 70, RSI(21) below 25
SMA — SMA 20 crosses above/below SMA 50
EMA — EMA 12 crosses above/below EMA 26
MACD — MACD crosses above/below signal
Bollinger — price above upper / below lower band
Golden/Death Cross — SMA 50/200 crossover
Volume — volume above 2x average
Price vs MA — price above/below SMA/EMA

⚠️ Risk management (optional, add at end)

Add stop loss 5% and/or take profit 15% to any strategy. Also works: SL 3%, TP 10%
