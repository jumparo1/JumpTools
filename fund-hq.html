1 Discover — Find Projects

Go to Market Data and search for any crypto or stock.

Each search result shows 3 quick actions:

  • + Watch — add to your watchlist for live tracking
  • + Dump — save to Project Dump for later review
  • AI Report — instantly generate a research report

Crypto uses CoinGecko (free, no key). Stocks need a free FMP key in Settings.

2 Score — Automatic Ratings

Every asset gets a 5-star rating computed from live fundamentals.

Crypto scores: Momentum, Liquidity, Value (dilution), Market Position, Recovery (ATH distance).

Stock scores: Valuation (P/E), Profitability (ROE), Health (D/E), Momentum (52W range), Yield.

Stars appear on watchlist cards and in the research workspace. When AI research runs, it can add a ±0.5★ qualitative adjustment.

3 Research — Templates & AI

Go to Templates and pick a framework:

  • Crypto Research — 18 sections (tokenomics, team, moats, whales, etc.)
  • Stock Research — 10 sections (financials, valuation, DCF, etc.)
  • Quick Thesis — 5 sections for fast screening

Each section shows a prompt (what to write) and bullet points (what to cover).

Two ways to fill: write manually, or click 🤖 AI to let Claude research it. You can also "Run All with AI" or "Fill Empty Only".

4 Report — Save & Review

When your research is done, click Save as Report to compile all sections into a formatted report.

Reports are saved to Firebase and appear in the Reports page. You can:

  • Set a Rating (Strong Buy, Buy, Hold, Sell, Avoid, etc.)
  • Set Conviction (High, Medium, Low)
  • Edit the content anytime
  • Re-run AI research with a different model

All reports sync in real-time between users via Firebase.

5 Track — Watchlist Monitor

Your Market Data watchlist tracks live prices, 24h changes, key metrics, and scores.

Click any card to see the full detail view with all metrics and the score breakdown.

Hit ↻ Refresh All to update prices and re-compute scores for everything at once.

💬 Pamela — Chat Assistant

Click the chat bubble (bottom-right) to open Pamela, your fund assistant. She can:

  • price btc — check any crypto/stock price
  • watch eth — add to watchlist
  • research sol — generate an AI research report
  • Navigate to any page, search projects, and more
